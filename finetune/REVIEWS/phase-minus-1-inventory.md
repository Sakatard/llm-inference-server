# Phase -1 — Dataset Inventory (locked decisions)

## Primary raw sources

### SII-WANGZJ/Polymarket_data (MIT) — POLYMARKET PRIMARY

**Use:** trade-flow + market-metadata.

**Files we'll pull:**
| File | Size | Records | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| `markets.parquet` | 85 MB | 538,587 markets | Market metadata (question, rules, condition_id, event_id, outcomes, resolution). Cheap. Pull entire file. |
| `trades.parquet` | 28 GB | 418.3 M trades | Trade events with `timestamp`, `market_id`, `price` (0-1), `usd_amount`, `nonusdc_side` (YES/NO). Pull selectively by `market_id` filter or stream. |

**Schema highlights for our pipeline:**
- `market_id` joins to news (we'd derive a question-string-search query from `markets.parquet` to feed GDELT/Google RSS).
- `price` at `timestamp` reconstructs the mid-price at any decision time.
- `usd_amount` × `nonusdc_side` lets us derive crude bid/ask imbalance over a window (no full CLOB depth but workable approximation).

**Skip:** `orderfilled.parquet` (84 GB raw blockchain — we have processed `trades.parquet`), `quant.parquet` (normalized YES-perspective — not needed for our v0), `users.parquet` (per-trader behavior — not needed).

**Disk budget:** ~28 GB for trades + 85 MB markets.

### trentmkelly/polymarket_crypto_derivatives (CC-BY-SA-4.0 — ACCEPTED) — CRYPTO PRIMARY

**Use:** native per-step orderbook snapshots for BTC/ETH/SOL/XRP up-down markets.

**Files per market episode:**
| File | Records | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| `steps.parquet` | 1 row per decision snapshot | step_index, ts, chainlink_price, binance_price, best_bid/best_ask/mid/spread/imbalance for UP and DOWN tokens. **This IS our decision-moment input for crypto.** |
| `events.parquet` | 1 row per CLOB event | event_type, ts, price, size, side. Lets us reconstruct flow between decision steps. |
| `book_levels.parquet` | full order book levels at each snapshot | Full CLOB depth — feeds the microstructure agent. |

**Time coverage:** 2026-02-21 through 2026-03-24 UTC. ~31 days, narrow window. We treat the entire dataset as in-scope.

**Decision-time selector (per SPEC §4a):** native per-step snapshot, one per market seeded by `market_id`.

**License attribution required:** "Polymarket Crypto Derivatives Order Book Data — trentmkelly — CC-BY-SA-4.0" in adapter metadata + LICENSE-ATTRIBUTION.md.
